About the Author

American mathematician Cody Griffith was born and raised in Denver, Colorado. He received his BSc. from Metropolitan State University of Denver and his MSc. from the University of British Columbia, Vancouver. Following some international travels, he worked as a developer in San Jose, California for several years before realizing it was time to shift his life and career goals to Germany!

Facing an assortment of troubles with immigrating during the SARS CoV2 pandemic, Cody decided to form this blog to help others wanting to do something similar. Here you will find the process explained in plain English and resources that you can reach out to for help.
